Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our headphones

What is the actual battery life?

The battery life of up to 30 hours applies when using the headphones at about 50% volume without active noise cancellation. With ANC activated, you can expect around 24-26 hours of playback time. The headphones support fast charging - 15 minutes of charging provides up to 5 hours of playback.

How effective is the noise cancellation?

Our advanced noise cancellation technology reduces ambient noise by up to 30dB, effectively eliminating background noises like airplane engines, traffic, air conditioning, and office chatter. The adaptive technology automatically adjusts to your environment for optimal performance in any situation.

Can I use them for phone calls?

Absolutely! The headphones feature a professional-grade microphone array that provides crystal clear voice pickup. The beamforming technology isolates your voice from background noise, ensuring you sound clear on every call. These headphones are perfect for conference calls and voice assistants.

What devices are compatible?

The headphones are compatible with any Bluetooth-enabled device including smartphones (iPhone and Android), tablets, laptops, and even some smart TVs. They also come with a 3.5mm audio cable for wired connections. The companion app is available for both iOS and Android.

What is included in the box?

Each package includes: (1) Premium Wireless Headphones, (1) Premium hard-shell carrying case, (1) USB-C charging cable, (1) 3.5mm audio cable, quick start guide, and warranty information. The carrying case has a dedicated compartment for all accessories.
