Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tank tops

Our tank tops feature a proprietary seamless built-in bra design that provides comfortable support without the discomfort of underwires. The lightweight, moisture-wicking fabric combined with the loose-fitting silhouette creates a top that's perfect for both workouts and casual wear.

We provide a detailed size chart that matches UK sizes with weight ranges for the perfect fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a looser fit or sizing down for more support. Remember all our tank tops are designed with stretch material for comfortable flexibility.

For best results, machine wash cold on gentle cycle with like colors and tumble dry low. Avoid bleaching or ironing directly on printed areas. The high-quality fabric maintains its shape and color through multiple washes when cared for properly.

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, simply return the unworn tank top with original tags for a full refund or exchange. Return shipping is free within the US. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

US orders typically ship within 1 business day and arrive in 3-5 business days via USPS. International shipping times vary by destination but typically take 10-20 business days. All customers receive tracking information once their order ships.
